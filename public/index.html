About Lily

LSD

More Than Just a Pretty Face

Lily St. Drake is a professional drag artist and entertainer who brings a unique blend of glamour, comedy, and mind-bending performance art to every stage. With the intentional nod to the psychedelic experience, Lily's performances are designed to take audiences on a journey they won't forget.

From high-energy club performances to sophisticated corporate events, Lily delivers unforgettable entertainment that pushes boundaries and breaks expectations. Every show is a unique experience, a chance to escape reality and enter a world of color, music, and pure entertainment.

With a growing following and a reputation for professionalism, Lily St. Drake is quickly becoming one of the most sought-after drag artists in the scene.
