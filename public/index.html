ABOUT

LILY ST. DRAKE

Lily St. Drake is a professional drag artist known for her electrifying performances and unique aesthetic. Blending high-fashion glamour with avant-garde creativity, LSD creates unforgettable experiences that push the boundaries of drag artistry.

With a signature style that channels acid elegance and neon dreams, Lily brings a fresh perspective to the drag scene. Her performances are a journey through visual storytelling, combining stunning costumes, dynamic choreography, and magnetic stage presence.

Based in Louisville KY, Lily is available for performances, events, and collaborations. Take a trip you'll never forget.
